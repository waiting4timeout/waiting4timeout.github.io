  1. Drag the green button below to your browser's bookmarks bar
  2. Open a ChatGPT conversation you want to export
  3. Click the bookmark — it scrolls the full conversation automatically and downloads a .md file

What happens when you click the bookmark

  1. Auto-scrolls the conversation from top to bottom, waiting for each batch of messages to load
  2. Collects every message as it scrolls — handles ChatGPT's lazy loading so no messages are missed
  3. Converts to Markdown preserving code blocks, headings, bold, italic, and links
  4. Downloads a .md file named after the conversation title and today's date
The script runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server — your conversation stays private.
Works on chatgpt.com in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
